Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?
Timeline depends on project scope. A standard website takes 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal phase.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Absolutely. We offer various maintenance packages to ensure your software stays secure, updated, and performs optimally after launch.
How do you handle project communication?
We use modern tools like Slack, Jira, and weekly video calls to keep you updated. You'll have a dedicated project manager as your primary point of contact.
